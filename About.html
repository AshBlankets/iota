What is iota?

News Feed


The future of healthcare is here. Iota is a collaborative healthcare system run by HealthCorp.

Just register here and choose a subscription plan. You will be sent a series of pills containing millions of microscopic robots we call ‘iomites’

These are programmed to access and eradicate harmful organisms. You can monitor the status of your treatment by logging in and viewing your profile.

Who can use it?


Anyone can use iota. We cater to all levels of sickness, from the common cold to cancer.

Parents can sign their children up. Doctors can sign their patients up. They both can then monitor the status and progress of the iomites nanobots from the status page.
