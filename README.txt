How much trust would you put in a company that could save your life?
Would you give them the power to kill and trust them not to?

Iota is a collaborative healthcare system. The newest innovation in medical technology.
It can be used to effectively treat most illnesses including viruses, bacterial infections, skin conditions, and even cancer.

The patient registers via our website www.iota.healthcorp.com and chooses a subscription plan. We send you the treatment, a series of pills containing millions of microscopic robots or ‘iomites’ We have programmed these to access and eradicate harmful organisms. Our staff regularly monitor their status and you can check progress yourself by logging in to your account.
